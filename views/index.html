About the Reviewer

The author of Passable Beer Reviews hates to admit it now, but after his first sip of beer, a Natural Ice, he had two thoughts: “Hey, this is pretty good,” and, “I like beer.” This notion, like many others he has had during the ensuing *17* years, was half right. The author, now a 38-year-old husband and father, still likes beer — almost all of it. He started Passable Beer Reviews to separate the inoffensive adjunct lagers from those that should be shunned by polite society, like Natty Ice. To learn more about his personal beer preferences, visit the favorite beers page dedicated to the topic.
