About the Reviewer

The author of Passable Beer Reviews hates to admit it now, but after his first sip of beer he had two thoughts: "Hey, this Natural Ice is a good beer," and "I think I like beer." He was, as usual, half right. The author, now a 38-year-old husband and father, does like beer. He started Passable Beer Reviews to help fellow beer-likers separate the merely inoffensive adjunct lagers from those that should be shunned by polite society, like Natty Ice. To learn more about his personal beer preferences, visit the favorite beers page dedicated to the topic.
